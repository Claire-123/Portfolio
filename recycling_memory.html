Interactive Installation • 2025

Recycling Memory

Overview

Explores the fragility of our memory. By revisiting the nostalgic technology, the project reflects on how we store, recall and ultimately lose parts of ourselves. Created with Arduino, p5.js, and Blender.

  • Arduino
  • p5.js
  • Blender (3D Rendering)

Research Phase

My journey started with the feeling of 'digital decay'—how memories shift when stored in 3D space. I iterated through various conceptual models before landing on this final proposal.

Process

Prototyping involved syncing Arduino sensor data with p5.js to trigger the 3D rendered Blender videos in real-time.
